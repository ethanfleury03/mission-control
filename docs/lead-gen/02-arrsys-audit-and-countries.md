# arrsys.com audit and country analysis

## Product lines and technical spec signals

Product structure clusters into:

- **Digital label printers** (narrow web and wide web, water-based pigment; hybrid; some UV).
- **Print-and-cut inline** (e.g. Nova: printing + laser die cutting).
- **Digital label finishing** (laser, blade, slitters/rewinders).
- **Flexible packaging** — substrate range (PP, PET, PVC, BOPP, Mylar, Tyvek), variable printing, food-compliant inks.
- **Industrial/security printing and variable data** — UV inks, serialization, QR, anti-counterfeiting.
- **Materials** — Arrow-branded label/flexible packaging films.

## Industries served

**“Industries that we serve”** groupings include: Aircraft Interior, Beverages Labels, Building Materials, Chemical Label Printing, Flexible Packaging, Government & Military, Label Packaging Printing.

Homepage clusters: Food and Beverages Labels, Pharma & Nutraceuticals, Aviation, Industrial & Chemical Labels, Wallpaper and Decor.

**Use-case highlights from industry pages**

- **Beverage labels:** variable data (QR/barcodes), intuitive software.
- **Pharmaceutical:** water-based pigmented ink as safety; labeling growth.
- **Chemical:** **GHS** labeling; durable aqueous inks.
- **Government & military:** GHS-certified equipment; **GSA** vendor positioning.
- **Aircraft interiors:** in-house placards/graphics; FAA compliance themes.
- **Building materials / decor:** ceramic & fiber cement; zero setup, fast changeovers; ~7-color, ~400 dpi.
- **Corrugation:** e-commerce packaging; OverJet Pro 700; water-based, inline varnish.

## Buyer personas and purchase triggers

**Personas:** brand owners/manufacturers in-house; label converters (short runs, prototyping); packaging (VDP, serialization, anti-counterfeit); compliance-heavy (food, pharma, chemicals, aviation); government procurement.

**Triggers:** SKU proliferation / high mix / short runs; fast turnaround; sustainability / safer ink chemistry; traceability and security; workflow completeness (print + cut + finishing).

## ICP constraints (hard or strong filters)

- **Facility:** three-phase power, compressed air, temp/humidity (e.g. Hybrid Pro M: **400V AC + N+E**, **6 bar**, **20–30°C**, humidity limits).
- **Substrate/application:** PP, PET, PVC, BOPP, Mylar, Tyvek, papers — maps to system class.
- **Compliance:** Nestlé Guidance, Swiss Ordinance, GHS, FAA themes by segment.
- **Width/format:** narrow vs wide vs corrugated width requirements.
